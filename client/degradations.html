Step by step guide:

I - Upload an image (supported formats are JPEG, JPG, PNG, TIFF and TIF).

II - Choose your degradation :
  • GrayScale Character Degradation
  • Shadow Binding
  • Phantom Character
  • Bleed Through
  • Blur Filter

III - Once you are happy with yours degradations, you can download the modified image at anytime !

IV - You can extract the font to your image directly from the menu Font Creator .
