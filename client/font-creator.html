    Help

    Step by step guide:

    I - Upload an image (supported formats are JPEG, JPG, PNG, TIFF and TIF).

    II - Select a letter with your mouse. It will appear in the Letter Preview field. If you are not happy with the native selection, you can correct the baseline and the different border lines.

    III - It is possible to merge several selected elements in the Letter Assignment field. For example, if the upper and the lower part of an "i" are detected separately, you can merge them holding ctrl on windows/linux and cmd on mac while selecting both parts.

    IV - Once you are happy with your character, you can assign it (both letter and unicode are accepted) and save it in the Letter Assignment field.

    V - The Alphabet section shows all the letters already extracted.

    VI - The Options section allows you to zoom in/out and reset zoom on original position. You also can show or unshow the baseline, start again with a new image and download your font.

    VII - You can add degradations to your image directly from the menu Degradations . It is possible to download the modified image at anytime !
